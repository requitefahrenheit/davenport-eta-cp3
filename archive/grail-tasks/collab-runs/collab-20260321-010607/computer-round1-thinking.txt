Here's what the fiber analysis reveals: the shadows from different fibers have zero overlap at weights below p, but complete overlap at weight p itself. What's striking is how many vectors in the full shadow can only be reached by mixing both fibers. At weight 4 with p=5, that's 371 out of 619 vectors. At weight 6 with p=7, it's 1706 out of 2389.
