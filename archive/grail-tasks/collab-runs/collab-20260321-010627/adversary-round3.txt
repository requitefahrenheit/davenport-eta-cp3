### Bypass attempt B: Fiber induction without uniqueness

Project B to F_p^r. Each fiber has ≤ α(r) nonzero tails (by rank-r η bound). To prove B has full shadow:

For target (c, t) with c ≠ 0, we need t in the "cross-fiber shadow." This requires combining partial sums from different fibers.
