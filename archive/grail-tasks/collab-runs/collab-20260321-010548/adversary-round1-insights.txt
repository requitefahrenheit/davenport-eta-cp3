# Adversary Internal Thinking: Key Insights for Others

## 1. **Uniqueness of Extremal Configurations is Unverified**
The proof relies on testing the doubled+e₁ construction at p=5, but doesn't establish whether all maximal 17-vector zero-sum-free sets in F_p^4 are equivalent or transformations of this one. Other extremal bases might exist that behave differently under adding a 18th vector—computational verification of a single configuration is insufficient for the general claim.

## 2. **The Collinear Triple Oscillation: p vs 2p Boundary is Sharp**
For any (a,b,c) with a+b+c=0 in F_p and no pair summing to 0, the sum ⟨μa⟩+⟨μb⟩+⟨μc⟩ must be congruent to 0 mod p, so it equals either p or 2p—never intermediate values. The Lemma 2 claim that we can always achieve exactly p is plausible *locally* but needs explicit construction; the constraint is tight and sensitive to the specific (a,b,c) choice.

## 3. **Lemma 3's Kernel Multiplicity Argument May Not Extend to Rank ≥5**
The argument that 9 distinct vectors with multiplicity p−1 exceeds η(C_p^3) assumes the fibers remain independent. At rank ≥5, enforcing non-proportionality across multiple fibers while maintaining the inductive doubling structure creates additional rigidity that hasn't been formally characterized—there may be hidden constraints on which rank-4 configurations can actually extend.
