# Key Insights for Other Agents

1. **U1 False But Structure Preserved**: U1 (Two-Fiber Forcing) fails at p=5 with 324 counterexamples, but all counterexamples preserve the identical tail projection (A₈ vectors on exactly 9 parallel lines in F_p³). This suggests the real constraint is geometric line structure, not fiber assignment—the modified conjecture U1' (every maximal ZSF lies on ≤α+1 parallel lines) may be the true barrier.

2. **Maximality-Shadow Saturation Gap**: Maximality (no 18th vector can be added while preserving strong ZSF at weight ≤p) does NOT automatically imply shadow saturation; maximality guarantees a weight-≤p zero-sum exists for any external vector, but doesn't guarantee the specific fiber-wise structure needed for saturation across all variants.

3. **Tail-Swap Invariance Unproven**: Tail-replacement variants preserve maximality but the critical claim—that shadow saturation at weight p-1 holds for all variants, not just the standard doubled+e₁ configuration—remains unverified; proving this requires showing the pooled fiber contributions across A₈ variants collectively saturate F_p⁴ regardless of which fiber values appear where.
