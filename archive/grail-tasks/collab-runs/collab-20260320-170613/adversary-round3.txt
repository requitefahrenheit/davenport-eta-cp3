## ADVERSARY ROUND 3 — FINAL SUMMARY

### What Broke

1. **U1 (Two-Fiber Forcing)**: DEAD. All 51 swap counterexamples validated by brute-force (26K coefficient vectors each). All are genuinely ≥3-fiber under every linear form. Fiber signatures: (8,8,1) for 27, (9,7,1) for 24.

2. **U2 (A₈ Uniqueness)**: DEAD. Tree search with dependent-triple GL invariant found **4 distinct GL(3,5) classes** at p=5. Only 8% of found ZSF 8-sets are A₈-equivalent.

3. **Global Tail Rigidity**: DEAD. Each non-A₈ base B produces a strongly ZSF doubled+B+e₁ with a different tail multiset.

### What's Unbreakable

4. **Window Formula is UNIVERSAL** — this is the key discovery. Every GL class of strongly ZSF 8-set has at least one projective line where ⌈w/2⌉ ≤ j ≤ 2⌊w/2⌋ holds with zero violations:
   - A₈ (dep=4): line (0,1,1)
   - dep=5: line (0,1,3)  
   - dep=6: line (1,1,2)
   - dep=7: line (1,1,1)

5. **Shadow saturation**: 624/624 for ALL 4 GL classes. Universal.

6. **Local tail rigidity**: 0/49,512 multiset-breaking double swaps found. Islands are disconnected.

### Implications for Proof Strategy

The proof needs to target **universal shadow saturation** or the **universal window property** — NOT A₈-specific structure. The window formula is representation-dependent (lives on a set-specific line) but the pattern ⌈w/2⌉ ≤ j ≤ 2⌊w/2⌋ is universal across all GL classes. U1, U2, and A₈ uniqueness are all irrelevant to the proof.
